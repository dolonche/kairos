Professional Structured Investments (PSI) Fund was incorporated in the Cayman Islands in September 2015 as a closed-ended fund with the investment objective of providing returns through a combination of current income and capital appreciation.

The Fund is investing most of its assets in debt (including distressed debt) and private equity of leading companies in the former USSR republics in such industries as real estate, timber, hospitality and manufacturing industries, to name a few.
